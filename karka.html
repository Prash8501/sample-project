Jupiter will transit in the eighth house from your zodiac till April. Diabetes patients should pay attention to their eating habits and diet.

FINANCIAL CONDITION:

You might have monetary gains from your family members. Your financial condition will remain good. You will have good earnings from your business. You might also receive some beautiful presents. At the beginning of the year, Rahu will be in your eleventh house and thus provide you profitable opportunities from various sources. FAMILY AND SOCIAL LIFE:

Your family members will respect your feelings. You will be happy with the daily activities and pursuits of your children. Your children will achieve significant success in their careers. You will be less inclined towards social engagements. You will have cordial relationships with your father and you will always have his backing and support. The transit of Ketu in the fourth house might adversely affect your mother’s health. Post April, you might do family planning.

LOVE LIFE:

Your married life will remain pleasant. You might propose marriage to your partner. Those who want a love marriage should get married at the beginning of the year. You might also go on a romantic getaway during mid-year. You will be very possessive about your life partner. Taking advice from your life partner will benefit you.

EDUCATION AND CARRER :

Students will be eager to learn new things and develop new skills. Jupiter’s transit in April will motivate you to explore new dimensions of education. Engineering students will pass their exams with flying colours. The period from May to July is highly favourable for those who will be appearing in recruitment drives. There would be work pressure on people associated with the banking sector. Your work quality will increase and earn you praise and appreciation. Avoid taking debts.
