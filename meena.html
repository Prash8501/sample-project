Pisces Sign

HEALTH :

Till April, your health will remain excellent. During the mid-year, respiratory and liver-related problems may trouble you. The condition of arthritis patients may exacerbate. Avoid taking stress, especially during the year-end. Heart patients may face some health-related problems. You may need money for the treatment of diseases. Remain careful during journeys. Those who live in cold regions may face health troubles. Along with your regular treatment, alternative medicines like Ayurvedic and Homeopathic treatment may also benefit you. Practice Yoga to keep your mind calm. Remain careful about your health especially in June, September and December.

FINANCIAL CONDITION:

This year, you will try to control your unnecessary expenses. You may purchase new properties. You will be able to negotiate efficiently and may get some excellent deals on expensive items. Those who are still waiting to buy their first house may have their dream come true this year. You may buy expensive cars. The time period till April will be most favourable for your financial condition. During the mid-year, you may end up incurring losses in order to show-off your wealth. Rahu’s transit in the second house might provoke you to earn money from illegal ways. You may buy some important electronic accessories. Saturn will keep you debt-free this year.

FAMILY AND SOCIAL LIFE:

This year, your family may conduct religious and auspicious ceremonies. It would be good to take the permission of your family before starting a new work. You will actively lead all the family events. Your social circle will widen. There might be problems among relatives at the year-end. People may ridicule your goodness. You will be delighted and proud of your children’s behaviour. There are chances of auspicious events in your family in February and March. The health of an elderly family member will be a matter of concern between July and September.

LOVE LIFE:

Husbands and wives will have better understanding and harmony in their relationship. There are strong chances of the marriage of unmarried people. You may propose to your love partner. You may develop new love relationships in April and May. You will remain loyal in your love relationship. But still, there would be hindrances in your path of love. Remain patient and you will be able to overcome any undesirable situations. You may visit a hill station with your life partner. Overall, this year is highly favourable for your marital relationship.

EDUCATION AND CARRER :

Students may face hindrances in their education. But you will persevere with your willpower to overcome every obstacle. You may take admission in a new course. The year is favourable if you are planning to go abroad for studies at the beginning of the year. The year is also favourable from a career’s perspective. Jupiter’s transit in its own Rashi will be especially propitious for those who are preparing for civil services. Your managers will wholeheartedly appreciate your work efficiency. There are chances of promotion in the second half of the year. You may start a new business in September. New partners may join your business. After April, people associated with politics may get promoted to a higher position.
