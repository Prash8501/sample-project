HEALTH :

This year, you will need to remain careful about your health. Even the slightest negligence about health will cost you dearly. At the beginning of the year, there are chances of an injury from iron. So, remain cautious in this regard. In June and July, some skin-related problems and bile disorders may trouble you. Diabetes patients will need to make active efforts to improve their lifestyle. Those who rely on the insulin dose should practice Yoga and try natural therapy along with their regular medicines. Take special care of your health in September and October.

FINANCIAL CONDITION:

This year, you will succeed in improving your lifestyle by acquiring means of luxury and comfort. You may invest in various policies to secure your future. During the first quarter of the year, Jupiter’s transit in your sixth house will encourage you to spend money on various things. You may also spend lavishly on weddings. You may purchase expensive jewelry. You will also earn money through various tours. After April, there would be a significant increase in your income.

FAMILY AND SOCIAL LIFE:

You might receive some bad news from your distant relatives. You might be somewhat self-conscious on the social front. You will succeed in establishing a unique self-image. Your siblings will remain supportive. Your children may disappoint you. You will remain serious about the upbringing and education of your children.

LOVE LIFE:

Mutual harmony and understanding would be good in your marital relationship. You will feel intense affection for your love partner. The marriage of unmarried people may get fixed this year. You may go on a vacation at the beginning of the year. In the mid-year, there would be tiffs between partners over petty issues. But you will sensibly resolve them. Your life partner might achieve big success in their career.

EDUCATION AND CARRER :

Those who are trying to take admission for higher studies will get excellent results in their endeavours. You may get a chance to work on new projects. Your self-confidence will be widely admired. Your performance will improve at the workplace. Between April and September, you might get a good job. This year, you will get excellent results in your career. You will be satisfied with your work methodology. You should avoid switching jobs in the months of October and November. Avoid making big investments in the stock market.
