Sagittarius Sign

HEALTH :

If you can maintain a balanced daily routine, your health will remain good this year. But if you are fond of heavy food, then you may develop health conditions like obesity and diabetes. You may come down with a high fever in the months of April and May. You may also complain of excessive heat and burning in the stomach. You should drink an adequate amount of water. Adding seasonal fruits to your diet will keep you healthy. In August, protect yourself from diseases like flu and typhoid.

FINANCIAL CONDITION:

Your financial condition will remain good this year. Till April, you may invest money in some big projects. You may also purchase new properties. But you will also be prone to wasting money unnecessarily. There are chances of sudden gains as well as sudden losses in the stock market. You will make efforts to develop alternative income sources. Working professionals may expect a salary hike by the year-end. You will repay all your debts at the beginning of the year.

FAMILY AND SOCIAL LIFE:

There will be peace and prosperity in your family at the beginning of the year. People expect a lot from you and you will need to work extra hard to fulfill their expectations. Your mother’s health will be worrisome during the mid-year. Take full care of your mother’s health from March to July. The health of your father will remain good. You will spend substantially on the education of your children. You will have a cordial relationship with your maternal grandmother’s family. You will be appreciated for your keen judgments and decisions.

LOVE LIFE:

Your life partner will give you emotional support. Unmarried people may get married in the first three months. Communication will improve between partners. You may get in trouble for placing blind trust in your love partner. Young girls should remain a little cautious regarding their love relationship. During the mid-year, you may face huge problems about your potential love marriage. Nevertheless, you will fully enjoy love and romance this year. The months of June and November will be somewhat unfavourable for your marital relationship. You may go on a romantic getaway during monsoon season.

EDUCATION AND CARRER :

At the beginning of the year, you may decide to switch your job. Your managerial skills will be acknowledged. Those who want to go abroad will overcome all obstacles and succeed in their endeavours. People associated with cosmetics and beauty products; and online retailing businesses will register a significant increase in their income. Post April, you will be able to recover all the previous business losses. Most of the year will be favourable from the career’s perspective. Those who are pursuing a career in the agriculture sector will get excellent opportunities. Prepare a little harder if you will be appearing in exams in May. Your managers will be happy with your work performance.
