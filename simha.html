Leo Sign

HEALTH :

The year won’t be much favourable from the health’s perspective. There would be continual ups and downs in your health. Your mental health would remain good though. You may come down with viral fever in March, so be careful. In October, liver-related problems may trouble you. Heart patients should take special care of their diet and eating habits. Tamasika diet will increase your blood pressure, so take it in moderation. Women natives may face reproductive system related problems at the beginning of the year. Jogging early morning will protect you from many diseases.

FINANCIAL CONDITION:

This year, you will have significant financial gains from the ancestral property. You might finalize a big property deal at the year-end. Working professionals may get a salary hike. Your expenses will decrease. But you may fail to manage your finances well. There might be financial losses in legal matters. Some people may ask to borrow money from you. The middle part of the year, from April to June, will be most favourable from the financial perspective. Your income might decrease between January and March.

FAMILY AND SOCIAL LIFE:

The year is favourable for your family life. Your family members will support you. You may plan the renovation of your house at the year-end. You will spend money to purchase expensive gadgets and accessories. You should behave appropriately with others, especially at the beginning of the year. After April, you will actively look after your family-related matters. In July and August, you might get embroiled in disputes. Some words or behaviour of your parents may hurt you.

LOVE LIFE:

The couples whose relationship is already going through a rough patch may face further conflicts in their married life. The period till March wouldn’t be favourable for your marital relationship. Post April, the tensions will reduce to some extent. Jupiter’s transit will cause ego clashes between partners. You will have financial gains from your in-laws’. Love partners may decide to marry. Your elders may get displeased with you. In July and August, you will be worried about your life partner’s health.

EDUCATION AND CARRER :

The year will remain favourable for businesspeople. Big orders will accelerate business growth. Your financial condition will become much stronger. The year is also favourable for people doing a job or business abroad. In the mid-year, working professionals may get a salary hike. You will receive special honours at the workplace. During the last three months, especially in November, you might face some difficulties in the job. Your boss will try to debase you. Don’t make friends with sycophants.
