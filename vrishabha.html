Taurus Sign

HEALTH :

During January and February, the weak position of your Rashi lord Venus will negatively affect your health. You may develop symptoms of confusion, primarily disorientation. After these two months, your health will remain good. But after August, you may face health-related problems due to a weak immune system. This year, you should not compromise with your sleep. Those who are suffering from conditions like insomnia might fall into depression. Practice Yoga and Pranayama daily and use organic products as much as you can. Avoid canned and junk food.

FINANCIAL CONDITION:

This year, your financial condition will remain strong. Although at the beginning of the year, you may face some money-related problems. After April, your income will increase. But your expenses also seem to accelerate at a double speed. You might have huge financial gains from stock market. You will find it difficult to save enough money. The period between April and September will be favourable from the financial perspective. Working professionals may get a salary hike.

FAMILY AND SOCIAL LIFE:

The year will remain favourable for your social position. Your dominance may decrease in your family. The beginning of the year hints at illnesses and troubles in your family. You may be under pressure due to your debts. After April, your circumstances will turn favourable. You will spend some quality time with your friends. Your friends will boost your enthusiasm. People will respect your thoughts and ideas. You will actively participate in charitable activities.

LOVE LIFE:

At the beginning of the year, Jupiter in your tenth house might cause tensions in your love relationship. You might be worried about your life partner’s health. All the tensions and conflicts will end with the transit of Jupiter in the eleventh house on April 13. You will spend quality time with your loved one. Those who have been single till now may get into a relationship this year. Your sexual activities might decrease at the end of the year. Don’t lose your temper on your life partner, especially in the month of November. The marriage of unmarried people may get fixed.

EDUCATION AND CARRER :

Children will perform brilliantly in their academics. If you are planning to appear in any exams this year, then there are high chances of success. Parents will closely monitor their children’s studies. You will try to learn new skills. Students who are studying abroad may face financial problems and troubles from the authority. Those who engage in stock market trading might earn huge profits. People associated with businesses related to electrical equipment; and travels might expand their businesses. The period post April would be especially favourable to you.
